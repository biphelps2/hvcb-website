Huon Valley Concert Band

The aim of the organisation is to provide:

  • a forum for musicians to regularly meet and play together
  • a quality community band to play at appropriate events in the Huon Valley and to represent the Huon Valley at events outside the area
  • opportunities for members of the Huon Valley community to receive basic music tuition (e.g.provide free instruments and tuition to beginners),
  • opportunities for members of the community to further their musical experience (e.g. arrange for professional musicians to conduct workshops for Huon Valley Musicians)

The Concert Band plays a variety of music including marches, movie themes, classical and pop.

To keep up to date with the latest news, see rehearsal time and place, or just to get in touch, please visit our Facebook page.
